Dubai

Something is dazzling, fabulous, and gleaming about Dubai.
It is a booming metropolis with great hustle and bustle
from where you never want to escape.

Key Highlights of Dubai

  • Iconic skyline with landmarks like the Burj Khalifa and Burj Al Arab.
  • Luxurious shopping destinations such as The Dubai Mall and Mall of the Emirates.
  • Stunning artificial islands, including Palm Jumeirah and The World.
  • Historic sites like the Dubai Creek and Al Fahidi Fort.
